### Paper Structure

Your final paper should include:

1. **Title Page** - Title, authors, affiliation
2. **Abstract** - 150-250 words summarizing your study
3. **Introduction** - Literature review and research question
4. **Method** - Data source, sample, procedure, analysis
5. **Findings** - Themes with supporting quotes
6. **Discussion** - Interpretation, HR recommendations, limitations, future directions
7. **References** - APA 7th edition format
8. **Tables** - Thematic summary table, HR policy recommendations table
